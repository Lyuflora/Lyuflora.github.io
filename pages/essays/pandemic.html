Pandemic Game Analyse

A board game

Fight with virulent diseases all over the world using 4 actions to travel between cities and take the responsibility of saving the infected populaces.

Pandemic is a strategy game with two to four players. In the game, players and teammates move characters between cities, eliminate local diseases, and develop antidotes at research stations. Players decide the actions of each round, cooperate to suppress the spread of diseases, deal with the increased viruses on the map, and also deal with manic outbreaks. Players have special abilities in the game, and using these abilities is an important part of winning together. It not only makes people feel a sense of excitement to master the destiny of human beings, but also organizes players to discuss cooperation. I think this is one of the reasons why Pandemic attracts me. One.

The four-movement modes are combined with the city card, which can produce many kinds of combined actions. Players combine several basic actions and use city cards for themselves or their teammates. There are many solutions to deal with events or cooperate with teammates, allowing players to come up with unique game strategies and gameplay. For example, there are 3 virus cubes in New York and Atlanta on the map. When players need to treat these two cities as soon as possible, they can make many decisions and choose the more favorable one. For example, nearby players use a little action point to go to Chicago, and then To clean up the virus in New York; or another player uses the city card of New York to go directly to New York to clean up the virus; or he can first meet with a companion who is a healer in a city, and give the other party a city card and let him go to Atlanta for treatment Go to treat New York by yourself while another disease.

The mechanism of drawing out the epidemic card makes every time a disease card is drawn, which causes an increase in the epidemic situation in some cities, and encourages players to actively clean up the virus. Every time it is drawn, it is more challenging. Once the pandemic card is drawn, the city cards that have been drawn will be put back on top of the deck. This drawing rule allows the infected city card to be drawn repeatedly. Every time a disease card is drawn, the disease block will be added. Delayed cleanup will make the situation even more difficult to deal with. In the beginning, the epidemic card will be shuffled in the player's deck. After it was drawn, the infection speed will be increased. The bottom of the infected card was drawn out, and the virus is added to 3, result in the broke out of the plague. Players need to shuffle the discarded infected card thoroughly and then put back. As a result, the cards of cities that have been infected will appear repeatedly, increasing the risk of "outbreaks" making the game more friendly to players at the beginning, and more challenging after a few rounds. Also, the mechanism of disease chain outbreak and accelerating spread speed of infections manipulates the difficulty and tension of the game.
