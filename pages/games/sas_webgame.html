Unmute

Theme "Keep it Alive"

The electric power has been cutted!! This place can’t live without the music and light, As a manager, you have to keep this place alive!! So let’s go, fix this problem by click or drag with your mouse!!

Rule

Insert the plug into the socket, and make the game scene alive again. The character and objects will react in response to the game sound effect you choose, such as "jump".

Operation

Drag and Drop the sound effects icons on any object~

Team: Celvin, Aris
Platforms: Microsoft Windows
Tools: Unity 3D
