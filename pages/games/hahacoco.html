HAHA & COCO

A 2D PLATFORM GAME

OVERVIEW

Summary

The global temperature is getting higher and higher, Haha, a dinosaur need to eat ice creams to decrease his body temperature, please help him!

> Play the game

Goal

Try your best to eat icecreams to survive! Keep your body temperature low!

Rule

  • Walk on the grass cube and eat ice creams to cool down.
  • Special ice creams have buffs that maintain a few seconds.
    • Eat a red spicy ice cream: get a buff -- you can shoot fireballs to clean the ice cream soup.
    • Eat a green sour ice cream: speed up.
  • When stepping on a melted ice cream, you will slip to the ground.

Operation

WSAD or ↑↓←→: change the direction and move SPACE: hold to jump J: shoot the fireballs (if you get fire buff) You can also use the virtual joystick and buttons on the screen to operate if you have a touch screen.
