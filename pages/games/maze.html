Gem Runner

3D Online Multiplayer Maze Game

OVERVIEW

Summary

Players search for gems to escape in the cave maze, collect powerful tricks, set traps for opponents, and find the exit of the maze in the melee.

Number of Players

1~4

Genre

Action, Multiplayer, Scavenger Hunt, Survival, Third-person Perspective

Story

In the 19th century, on Switch Island, countless explorers came to the culvert labyrinth just to find the legendary "new world". Not long ago, a mysterious person told them that there was a world of bliss at the end of the "door", with real magic, and everyone in that world owns invincible power. But to find and open the real exit, explorers need to go through many difficulties, to find the gems that open the door, and to guard against opponents' attacks. They can also attack actively to plunder gems from their opponents. All in all, only the strongest can be the first to open the door and gain recognition from the new world...

Game Design Details

How to play

Each player can move freely in the maze, collect materials to replenish their strength, find props to enhance their power, and gather certain gems to open the iron gate. When players meet, they can use props to attack each other; players can also place obstacles in the maze to obstruct later players.

Players collect gems throughout the maze and consume gems of the specified color and quantity when opening a door. Different doors have different requirements for gems (for example, some need 1 red + 2 green gems, and some need 2 blue gems).

WOW Factor

The core experience of the game is large-scale prank + peekaboo + treasure hunt. We plan to design a series of unique props and add multiple effects to them. The features are the funny effects brought about by the interaction between players. General games with "peek-a-boo" features are mostly FPS, and our game has no obvious long-range attack props, mainly by placing props and stealth approaching opponents to achieve attacks. In addition, we use a real sound effect system, the player can hear the opponent's approach while hiding.

Target Experience

  • Abundant character choices, unique missions and randomly generated props make every adventure full of freshness and new challenges.
  • Explore a large and complex maze that is carefully constructed, full of hidden areas and hidden passages.
  • Launch a large-scale "peek-and-see" with multiple players, become a ninja sneak attack, or put down a trap to make trouble.
  • Explore roles with different skills, and the use of props may produce unique effects.

Players in single-player mode can immerse themselves in the adventure of the maze and complete collection tasks.

Multiplayer mode players can indulge in the fun of multiplayer melee and the sense of accomplishment of successful sneak attacks.

Control

The player uses the arrow keys or WASD to move forward, backward, left, and right, the mouse controls the angle of view rotation, and the left button steals or opens doors or boxes to other players. Number keys to select props, Q key to use props, E key to view the backpack, space key to jump, C key to squat.

Mechanics

  • Racing: Multi-player online games, the fastest to reach the finish line wins.
  • Randomness: Food and props are randomly generated.
  • Traps: Players can open secret doors by looking for gems of various colors, and they will also have the opportunity to find various props during the period. Players can also use props to interfere with opponents and increase their probability of victory.
  • Stamina: The player's current character has a certain physical strength value. Walking and running will decrease stamina and player needs to find food to supplement it. When the stamina value drops to 30%, players must replenish their stamina in time, otherwise, they will face death threats. Once they die, the game ends.
