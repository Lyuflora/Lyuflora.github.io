Gunpoint Game Analyse

A black humor filled puzzle-platform game

Trick enemies and enjoy player's own playstyle: quick, quiet, non-violent, no living witnesses, or any combination.

Gunpoint is a stealth-based puzzle-platform video game. The protagonist played by the player is a hired assassin involved in a gun business conspiracy, sneaking into the building, dealing with guards, stealing information. The protagonist can jump up to the top of the building, jump down from the rooftop, or jump into the confidential office through the glass window. Gunpoint combines puzzle and action gameplay, with its dark humor, as a witty mediator.

The way to pass the game is very free. Players are encouraged to create new challenges by themselves, and additional tasks encourage players to use different strategies to pass the levels. Players only need to let the protagonist reach the goal, and will feel that creativity can have a lot of room for development. For example, on a guarded floor, the player wants to get the information of the computer room. He can use the spring-like mobility to hide like a ninja, waiting for the opportunity to jump on the ceiling and sneak into the room; he can also take the opportunity to fly down to the enemy and take away the secrets. Information; even when the patrolman opened the door while standing by the door, the enemy was stunned by the door panel. Moreover, the evaluation of the victory of "witnesses" and "making voices" makes players interested in thinking of a more perfect way to pass. The mechanism of extra tasks encourages players to try new ideas, such as "stealing information from a laptop" and "destroying all witnesses". Most of the time, players follow the same style and occasionally break the routine. This is usually very interesting. the process of.

Players can enter the electrician mode to connect different devices to cause a chain reaction, use the regular actions of the enemy to help them pass the level, and get a unique stealing and puzzle solving experience. Because the consequences of the circuit connection can affect the behavior of the enemy, players not only get feedback from the puzzle solving of the circuit connection, but also make the enemy perform funny behaviors. For example, players can get the skills to modify the circuit connection (Cross Link) and modify the shooting effect of the guards (Long shot), and they can design traps to induce the enemy to open the door for the protagonist, or they can be turned into a twitch by the socket around them when they pull the trigger. The player turns off the light, and then connects the logic of the switch to the door of the target room, so the patrol personnel will try to turn on the light, which instead solves the obstacle for the player.

The design of breaking glasses makes the player's strategy more varied and the experience more dramatic. When there is a glass block in the route, players who are cautiously pursuing safety must avoid the enemy’s vision, and plan the protagonist’s actions more carefully. Players who plan time and adventure carefully can break through three or four pieces of glass from the roof to go straight in. Breaking directly into the first floor, of course, will also cause a loud noise to attract more enemies.
