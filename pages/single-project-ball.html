Bouncing ball

A "clone" of To Hell with Johnny

This is a simple game build with OpenGL and C++.

RULE

Use ← → to spin the column and let the ball fall. Press ctr to zoom-in and zoom-out. Use ↑↓ to adjust perspective. If the ball falls on red tiles, then game over.
